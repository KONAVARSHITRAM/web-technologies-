IQOO NEO 10R FEATURES

Performance: Powered by the Snapdragon 8s Gen 3 processor paired with LPDDR5X RAM and up to 256GB of internal storage. It also includes a specialized Ultra Game Mode supporting stable 90FPS gaming and bypass charging.
Display: 6.78-inch 1.5K AMOLED display with a 144Hz gaming refresh rate and a peak brightness of up to 4500 nits.
Camera:
Rear: 50MP Sony primary sensor (with OIS) + 8MP ultra-wide lens.
Front: 32MP selfie camera.
Battery & Charging: 6400mAh Silicon-Carbon battery, backed by 80W FlashCharge technology.
Design & Durability: Ultra-slim profile (approx. 7.98mm) and an IP65 rating for dust and water resistance.
Operating System: Funtouch OS 15, based on Android 15.
Other Features: On-screen optical fingerprint scanner, dual stereo speakers, Wi-Fi 6, and Bluetooth 5.4
