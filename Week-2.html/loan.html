Loan Facilities & Partners.
Students can access education loans with the following typical parameters for VCE programs:
Lending Partners: The college collaborates with institutions like Capital India Finance Limited and other nationalized banks.
Loan Amounts:
Without Collateral: Up to ₹20 Lakhs for domestic studies.
With Collateral: No upper limit for domestic studies; up to ₹1 Crore for abroad studies (if applicable).
Interest Rates: Generally range between 10.5% and 14.5% per annum, depending on the lender and the applicant's profile.
Repayment Tenure: Typically 7 to 15 years, often including a moratorium period (course duration + 6 months) where only simple interest may be payable.
Coverage: Loans can cover tuition fees (approx. ₹1.45 Lakhs/year for B.Tech), hostel fees, and other miscellaneous charges.
