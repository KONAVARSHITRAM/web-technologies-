insurance

Vardhaman College of Engineering provides specific insurance coverage primarily for its employees as part of its welfare schemes, while student coverage is typically mandated through personal accident policies included in fees.
Staff Insurance & Welfare.
The college extends comprehensive welfare benefits to both teaching and non-teaching staff. Key components include:
Medical Insurance: Comprehensive health coverage is provided to employees.
Employee Provident Fund (EPF): Mandatory retirement savings contributions.
Additional Benefits: The institution also offers free transport, medical leave, maternity leave, and Child Care Leave (CCL).
Student Insurance
Personal Accident Insurance: A nominal fee component (often around ₹500–₹1,000 annually within the "Other Fees" category) typically covers a Personal Accident Insurance policy for all enrolled students.
Health Coverage: Students generally rely on personal health insurance or government schemes (like Aarogyasri in Telangana), though the college may facilitate group enrollment during admission. Specific group health plans for students are not explicitly detailed in public fee structures but are often optional or part of hostel packages.
